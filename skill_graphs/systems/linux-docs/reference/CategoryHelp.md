From ArchWiki
Welcome to the **Help** section. Here you will find resources and guidelines for ArchWiki [browsing](https://wiki.archlinux.org/title/Help:Browsing "Help:Browsing") and [reading](https://wiki.archlinux.org/title/Help:Reading "Help:Reading"), [discussion](https://wiki.archlinux.org/title/Help:Discussion "Help:Discussion"), and editing.
Browse this section, and get to know the ArchWiki if you want to help the community by creating or [translating](https://wiki.archlinux.org/title/Help:I18n "Help:I18n") content.
If you need assistance with other issues, see [ArchWiki:Contributing#Getting help](https://wiki.archlinux.org/title/ArchWiki:Contributing#Getting_help "ArchWiki:Contributing").
