#  Chapter 2. Public and Media Relations Planning
When you want to promote your organization, you are provided with a variety of options. The first step is to develop a plan. You must determine the following:
  * _What do you want to accomplish?_
  * _Who do you want to reachneeds to know about your organization?_
  * _What do you want to say??_
  * _What kind of information is newsworthy?_
