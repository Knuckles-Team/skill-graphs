# Working with domains
Last updated January 13, 2026
You can [buy a domain through Vercel](https://vercel.com/docs/multi-tenant#buying-a-domain-through-vercel) by going to the [Vercel.com domains page](https://vercel.com/domains) and using our fast search to [find one or more domains](https://vercel.com/docs/getting-started-with-vercel/buy-domain) that fit your brand and needs. The price of available domains is the same as the registrar's pricing and Vercel does not keep a log of your search history for marketing purposes.
