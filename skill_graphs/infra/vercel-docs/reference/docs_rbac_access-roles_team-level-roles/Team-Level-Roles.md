# Team Level Roles
Last updated January 7, 2026
Team level roles are available on [Enterprise](https://vercel.com/docs/plans/enterprise) and [Pro](https://vercel.com/docs/plans/pro) plans
Team level roles are designed to provide a comprehensive level of control and access to the team as a whole. These roles are assigned to individuals and are applicable to all projects within the team. This allows for a centralized level of control and access, while still maintaining the security and integrity of the team as a whole.
While the [Enterprise](https://vercel.com/docs/plans/enterprise) plan supports all the below roles, the [Pro](https://vercel.com/docs/plans/pro-plan) plan does not support the [Security](https://vercel.com/docs/rbac/access-roles#security-role) and [Contributor](https://vercel.com/docs/rbac/access-roles#contributor-role) roles.
