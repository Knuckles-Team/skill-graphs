##  [Email](https://vercel.com/docs/integrations/create-integration/submit-integration#email)[](https://vercel.com/docs/integrations/create-integration/submit-integration#email)
  * Required: Yes


There are two types of email that you must provide:
  * Contact email: This is the contact email for the owner of the integration. It will not be publicly visible and will only be used by Vercel to contact you.
  * Support contact email: The support email for the integration. This email will be publicly listed and used by developers to contact you about any issues.


As an integration creator, you are responsible for the support of integration developed and listed on Vercel. For more information, refer to [Section 3.2 of Vercel Integrations Marketplace Agreement](https://vercel.com/legal/integrations-marketplace-agreement). You are also solely responsible for your own compliance with applicable laws and regulations, including sanctions and export controls. See [Compliance and sanctions](https://vercel.com/docs/integrations/create-integration#compliance-and-sanctions) for more details.
