## Additional tools
###  [Azure Policy](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/governance/policy)
Enforce organizational standards and to assess compliance at-scale.
###  [Azure Developer CLI (azd)](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/developer/azure-developer-cli)
Use developer-friendly commands that map to key stages in your workflow (code, build, deploy, monitor).
###  [Azure Deployment Environments](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/deployment-environments)
Spin up app infrastructure with project-based templates that establish consistency and best practices while maximizing security.
Define, deploy, and collaborate on cloud-native applications across public clouds and private infrastructure.
