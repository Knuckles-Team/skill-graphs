# Microsoft Compliance
Microsoft documentation and resources you need to get started with compliance for your business or organization.

## Regulatory & industry compliance
Microsoft guidance on compliance with industry areas and international & domestic standards and regulations.
![](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/office/media/icons/compliance-blue.svg) [General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/compliance/regulatory/gdpr)
Guidance to help you honor rights and fulfill obligations under the GDPR when using Microsoft products and services.
![](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/office/media/icons/globe-internet.svg) [Microsoft compliance offerings](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/compliance/regulatory/offering-home)
Microsoft offers a comprehensive set of compliance offerings to help your organization comply with national, regional, and industry-specific requirements governing the collection and use of data.
![](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/office/media/icons/on-premises-blue.png) [Microsoft Cloud for industries](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/industry/)
Learn how Microsoft Azure, Dynamics 365, Microsoft 365, and Microsoft Power Platform can support compliance needs for your industry.
![](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/office/media/icons/task-checklist-planning-blue.png) [Australia and New Zealand compliance](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/compliance/anz/)
Learn how Microsoft can help support compliance requirements in Australia and New Zealand.
![](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/office/media/icons/bill-blue.png) [Digital Operational Resilience Act (DORA)](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/compliance/dora/dora-what-is-dora)
Learn how Microsoft is preparing for the upcoming EU Digital Operational Resilience Act and supporting our FSI customers.

## Get started with compliance in Microsoft online services
If you're new to compliance and wondering where to start with Microsoft online services, this section provides links to key compliance areas for each service.
###  [Microsoft Azure](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/compliance/)
Azure compliance provides you with compliance offerings, blueprints, compliance guides, and resources to help you meet your compliance obligations.
###  [Dynamics 365](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/dynamics365/business-central/compliance/compliance-overview)
Business Central contains functionality and is built on a platform that makes it easy for customers to comply with compliance and regulatory legislation.
###  [Microsoft Purview](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/purview/)
If your organization needs to comply with legal or regulatory standards, start here to learn about governance and compliance in Microsoft Purview.
