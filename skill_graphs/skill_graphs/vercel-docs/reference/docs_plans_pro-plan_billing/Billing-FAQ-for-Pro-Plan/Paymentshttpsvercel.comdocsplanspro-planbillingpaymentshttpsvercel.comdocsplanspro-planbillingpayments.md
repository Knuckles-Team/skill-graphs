##  [Payments](https://vercel.com/docs/plans/pro-plan/billing#payments)[](https://vercel.com/docs/plans/pro-plan/billing#payments)
###  [What is the price of the Pro plan?](https://vercel.com/docs/plans/pro-plan/billing#what-is-the-price-of-the-pro-plan)[](https://vercel.com/docs/plans/pro-plan/billing#what-is-the-price-of-the-pro-plan)
See the [pricing page](https://vercel.com/docs/pricing).
###  [When are payments taken?](https://vercel.com/docs/plans/pro-plan/billing#when-are-payments-taken)[](https://vercel.com/docs/plans/pro-plan/billing#when-are-payments-taken)
At the beginning of each [billing cycle](https://vercel.com/docs/plans/pro-plan/billing#what-is-a-billing-cycle). Each invoice charges for the upcoming billing cycle. It includes any additional usage that occurred during the previous billing cycle.
###  [What payment methods are available?](https://vercel.com/docs/plans/pro-plan/billing#what-payment-methods-are-available)[](https://vercel.com/docs/plans/pro-plan/billing#what-payment-methods-are-available)
Credit/Debit card only. Examples of invalid payment methods are gift cards, prepaid cards, EBT cards, and some virtual cards.
###  [What card types can I pay with?](https://vercel.com/docs/plans/pro-plan/billing#what-card-types-can-i-pay-with)[](https://vercel.com/docs/plans/pro-plan/billing#what-card-types-can-i-pay-with)
  * American Express
  * China UnionPay (CUP)
  * Discover & Diners
  * Japan Credit Bureau (JCB)
  * Mastercard
  * Visa


###  [What currency can I pay in?](https://vercel.com/docs/plans/pro-plan/billing#what-currency-can-i-pay-in)[](https://vercel.com/docs/plans/pro-plan/billing#what-currency-can-i-pay-in)
You can pay in any currency so long as the credit card provider allows charging in USD _after_ conversion.
###  [What happens when I cannot pay?](https://vercel.com/docs/plans/pro-plan/billing#what-happens-when-i-cannot-pay)[](https://vercel.com/docs/plans/pro-plan/billing#what-happens-when-i-cannot-pay)
When an account goes overdue, some account features are restricted until you make a payment. This means:
  * You can't create new Projects
  * You can't add new team members
  * You can't redeploy existing projects


For subscription renewals, payment must be successfully made within 14 days, else all deployments on your account will be paused. For new subscriptions, the initial payment must be successfully made within 24 hours.
You can be overdue when:
  * The card attached to the team expires
  * The bank declined the payment
    * Possible incorrect card details
    * The card is reported lost or stolen
  * There was no card on record or a payment method was removed


To fix, you can add a new payment method to bring your account back online.
###  [Can I delay my payment or be given an extension?](https://vercel.com/docs/plans/pro-plan/billing#can-i-delay-my-payment-or-be-given-an-extension)[](https://vercel.com/docs/plans/pro-plan/billing#can-i-delay-my-payment-or-be-given-an-extension)
No. Payments must be made upon invoice issuance. Delays and extensions are not available.
###  [Can I pay annually?](https://vercel.com/docs/plans/pro-plan/billing#can-i-pay-annually)[](https://vercel.com/docs/plans/pro-plan/billing#can-i-pay-annually)
No. Only monthly payments are supported. You can pay annually if you upgrade to an [Enterprise](https://vercel.com/pricing) plan. The Enterprise plan offers increased performance, collaboration, and security needs.
###  [Can I change my payment method?](https://vercel.com/docs/plans/pro-plan/billing#can-i-change-my-payment-method)[](https://vercel.com/docs/plans/pro-plan/billing#can-i-change-my-payment-method)
Yes. You will have to add a new payment method before you can remove the old one. To do this:
  1. From your [dashboard](https://vercel.com/dashboard), select your team in the team switcher
  2. Open Settings in the sidebar and select [Billing](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%7E%2Fsettings%2Fbilling&title=Go+to+Billing) from the left nav
  3. Scroll to Payment Method and select the Add new card button
