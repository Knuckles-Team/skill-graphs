##  [Limits](https://vercel.com/docs/vercel-firewall/vercel-waf#limits)[](https://vercel.com/docs/vercel-firewall/vercel-waf#limits)
Depending on your plan, there are limits for each Vercel WAF feature.
Feature | Hobby | Pro | Enterprise
---|---|---|---
[Project level IP Blocking](https://vercel.com/docs/security/vercel-waf/ip-blocking#project-level-ip-blocking) | Up to 10 | Up to 100 | Custom
[Account-level IP Blocking](https://vercel.com/docs/security/vercel-waf/ip-blocking#account-level-ip-blocking) | N/A | N/A | Custom
[Custom Rules](https://vercel.com/docs/security/vercel-waf/custom-rules) | Up to 3 | Up to 40 | Up to 1000
[Custom Rule Parameters](https://vercel.com/docs/security/vercel-waf/rule-configuration#parameters) | All | All | All
[WAF Managed Rulesets](https://vercel.com/docs/security/vercel-waf/managed-rulesets) | N/A | N/A | Contact sales
  * For Account-level IP Blocking, CIDR rules are limited to `/16` for IPv4 and `/48` for IPv6
  * For Custom Rule Parameters, JA3 (Legacy) is available on Enterprise plans
