# Vercel CMS Integrations
Last updated September 24, 2025
Vercel Content Management System (CMS) Integrations allow you to connect your projects with CMS platforms, including [Contentful](https://vercel.com/docs/integrations/contentful), [Sanity](https://vercel.com/marketplace/sanity), [Sitecore XM Cloud](https://vercel.com/docs/integrations/sitecore) and [more](https://vercel.com/docs/integrations/cms#featured-cms-integrations). These integrations provide a direct path to incorporating CMS into your applications, enabling you to build, deploy, and leverage CMS-powered features with minimal hassle.
You can use the following methods to integrate your CMS with Vercel:
  * [Environment variable import](https://vercel.com/docs/integrations/cms#environment-variable-import): Quickly setup your Vercel project with environment variables from your CMS
  * [Edit Mode through the Vercel Toolbar](https://vercel.com/docs/integrations/cms#edit-mode-with-the-vercel-toolbar): Visualize content from your CMS within a Vercel deployments and edit directly in your CMS
    * [Content Link](https://vercel.com/docs/edit-mode#content-link): Lets you visualize content models from your CMS within a Vercel deployments and edit directly in your CMS
  * [Deploy changes from CMS](https://vercel.com/docs/integrations/cms#deploy-changes-from-cms): Connect and deploy content from your CMS to your Vercel site
