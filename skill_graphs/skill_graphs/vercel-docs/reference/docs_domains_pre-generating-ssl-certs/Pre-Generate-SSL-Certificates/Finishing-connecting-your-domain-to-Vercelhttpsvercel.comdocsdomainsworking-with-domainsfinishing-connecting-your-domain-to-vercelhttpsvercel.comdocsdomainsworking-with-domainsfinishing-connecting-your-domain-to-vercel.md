##  [Finishing connecting your domain to Vercel](https://vercel.com/docs/domains/working-with-domains#finishing-connecting-your-domain-to-vercel)[](https://vercel.com/docs/domains/working-with-domains#finishing-connecting-your-domain-to-vercel)
To migrate your deployment to Vercel, add the provided A or CNAME record from your project’s Domain Settings page to your DNS configuration so your domain points to Vercel webservers. See [this detailed guide](https://vercel.com/kb/guide/a-record-and-caa-with-vercel) on using domains with A records for more information.
For more details on performing a migration, see [this guide](https://vercel.com/docs/domains/managing-dns-records#migrating-dns-records-from-an-external-registrar).
