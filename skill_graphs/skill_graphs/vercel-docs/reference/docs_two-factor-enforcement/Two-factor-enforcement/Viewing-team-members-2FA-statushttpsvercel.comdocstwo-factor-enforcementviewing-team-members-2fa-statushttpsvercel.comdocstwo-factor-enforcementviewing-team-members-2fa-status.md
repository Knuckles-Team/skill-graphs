##  [Viewing team members' 2FA status](https://vercel.com/docs/two-factor-enforcement#viewing-team-members'-2fa-status)[](https://vercel.com/docs/two-factor-enforcement#viewing-team-members'-2fa-status)
Team owners can view the two-factor authentication status of all team members in the [team members page](https://vercel.com/docs/rbac/managing-team-members). Users without 2FA will have a label indicating their state. A filter is available on the same page to show members with two-factor authentication enabled or disabled.
