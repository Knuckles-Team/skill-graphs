##  [Managed Infrastructure](https://vercel.com/docs/flags#managed-infrastructure)[](https://vercel.com/docs/flags#managed-infrastructure)
Vercel provides [Managed Infrastructure](https://vercel.com/products/managed-infrastructure) to deploy, scale, and secure your applications.
These resources are usage based, and billed based on the amount of data transferred, the number of requests made, and the duration of compute resources used.
Each product's usage breaks down into resources, with each one billed based on the usage of a specific metric. For example, [Function Duration](https://vercel.com/docs/functions/configuring-functions/duration) generates bills based on the total execution time of a Vercel Function.
###  [Managed Infrastructure billable resources](https://vercel.com/docs/flags#managed-infrastructure-billable-resources)[](https://vercel.com/docs/flags#managed-infrastructure-billable-resources)
Most resources include an amount of usage your projects can use within your billing cycle. If you exceed the included amount, you are charged for the extra usage.
See the following pages for more information on the pricing of each managed infrastructure resource:
  * [Vercel Functions](https://vercel.com/docs/functions/usage-and-pricing)
  * [Image Optimization](https://vercel.com/docs/image-optimization/limits-and-pricing)
  * [Edge Config](https://vercel.com/docs/edge-config/edge-config-limits)
  * [Web Analytics](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics/limits-and-pricing)
  * [Speed Insights](https://vercel.com/docs/speed-insights/limits-and-pricing)
  * [Drains](https://vercel.com/docs/drains#usage-and-pricing)
  * [Monitoring](https://vercel.com/docs/monitoring/limits-and-pricing)
  * [Observability](https://vercel.com/docs/observability/limits-and-pricing)
  * [Blob](https://vercel.com/docs/vercel-blob/usage-and-pricing)
  * [Microfrontends](https://vercel.com/docs/microfrontends#limits-and-pricing)
  * [Bulk redirects](https://vercel.com/docs/redirects/bulk-redirects#limits-and-pricing)


For [Enterprise](https://vercel.com/docs/plans/enterprise) pricing, contact our [sales team](https://vercel.com/contact/sales).
####  [Pro plan add-ons](https://vercel.com/docs/flags#pro-plan-add-ons)[](https://vercel.com/docs/flags#pro-plan-add-ons)
To enable any of the Pro plan add-ons:
  1. Visit the Vercel [dashboard](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2Fdashboard&title=Open+Dashboard) and select your team from the team switcher.
  2. Open Settings in the sidebar and go to [Billing](https://vercel.com/d?to=%2F%5Bteam%5D%2F%7E%2Fsettings%2Fbilling&title=Go+to+Billing).
  3. In the Add-Ons section, find the add-on you'd like to add. Switch the toggle to Enabled and configure the add-on as necessary.
