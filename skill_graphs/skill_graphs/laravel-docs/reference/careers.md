## Benefits

### Learn and grow
Work with the best and brightest in the Laravel ecosystem, building the tools that Laravel developers love.
![](https://laravel.com/build/assets/health-benefits-Dxxw0Ong.svg)
### Health benefits
Laravel is proud to offer robust, private health benefits no matter what country you reside in.
![](https://laravel.com/build/assets/life-work-balance-B1DTqUTH.svg)
### Remote workplace
We are a fully distributed team working together to build the next generation of Laravel packages and products.
![](https://laravel.com/build/assets/vacation-BqXQZecz.svg)
### Take a vacation
We believe well-rested employees do their best work. Laravel offers generous vacation time plus your country's holidays.
![](https://laravel.com/build/assets/perks-Bu4_l67F.svg)
### Retirement contributions
Retirement fund / pension contributions are available to Laravel employees, tailored to your country.

### Stock option eligibility
Laravel employees are eligible for stock options so that you share in Laravel's overall success.
