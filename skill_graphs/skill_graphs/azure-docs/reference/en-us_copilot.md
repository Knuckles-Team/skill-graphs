Welcome to Copilot learning hub. Here you can further your career and business using Microsoft Copilot, an AI-powered assistant leveraged within various products. This series will guide you in various uses of the Microsoft Copilot – adopting Copilot out-of-the-box, then extending Copilot on your own data or building your own Copilot. Copilot learning hub can be used as part of an Enterprise Skilling initiative as well as something you can do on your own.
