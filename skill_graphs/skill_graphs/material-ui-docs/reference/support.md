  * Please follow one of the issue templates provided on GitHub.

## [Technical support](https://mui.com/material-ui/getting-started/support/#technical-support)
We do _not_ offer paid support for Core libraries like Material UI.
But you might want to read the [technical support offered](https://mui.com/x/introduction/support/#technical-support) on MUI X components.
## [Long-term support (LTS)](https://mui.com/material-ui/getting-started/support/#long-term-support-lts)
Bug fixes, performance enhancements, and other improvements are delivered in new releases. However, we remain committed to providing security updates and addressing regressions for the immediate predecessor of the current major version.
This includes issues introduced by external sources, like browser upgrades or changes to upstream dependencies.

### [Discord](https://mui.com/material-ui/getting-started/support/#discord)
We have a [Discord Server](https://mui.com/r/discord/) to bring the Material UI community together. Our tools are used by thousands of developers and teams all around the world, many of whom actively engage with the community.
You can join Discord to engage in lively discussions, share your projects, and interact with developers just like you from all around the world. We'd love for you to join us!
How-to questions are not accepted on Discord, they should be asked on [Stack Overflow](https://mui.com/material-ui/getting-started/support/#stack-overflow).
## [Custom work](https://mui.com/material-ui/getting-started/support/#custom-work)
If your team gets stuck and needs help getting unblocked, Material UI's engineers may be available on a contract basis.
Keep in mind that the work must be directly related to Material UI's products‚Äîwe don't accept general web development or React work.
Our contracting price starts at $200/hour or $1,500/day.
## [Tidelift subscription](https://mui.com/material-ui/getting-started/support/#tidelift-subscription)
We and the maintainers of thousands of other packages work with Tidelift to deliver one enterprise subscription that covers all of the open-source you use.
If you want the flexibility of open-source and the confidence of commercial-grade software, this is worth looking at. The Tidelift Subscription manages your dependencies for you.
The end result? All of the capabilities you expect from commercial-grade software, for the full breadth of open-source you use. That means less time grappling with esoteric open-source trivia, and more time building your own applications‚Äîand your business.
